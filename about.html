Our Story

K.A.R.E Jewelry's design mission is to make accessories that will spice up your daily
looks effortlessly and timielessly, while also being kind to the Earth. Founded by four girls
studying at Tufts University in Boston, we specialize in minimalist and sustainable jewelry.
